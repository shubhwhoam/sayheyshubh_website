📋 BSc Zoology (Hons.) Semester 3 Syllabus Overview

📄 Download Official DU Syllabus PDF

The third semester of BSc Zoology (Hons.) introduces students to chordates, biochemistry, and human physiology. The core subjects include:

🐠 DSC-7 Diversity of Chordates: Includes Protochordata, Agnatha, Pisces, Amphibia, Reptilia, Aves, and Mammalia.
🧪 DSC-8 Biochemistry - Metabolic Processes: Covers Carbohydrate, Lipid, and Protein Metabolism, plus Oxidative Phosphorylation.
🫀 DSC-9 Human Physiology - Life Sustaining Systems: Focuses on Digestion, Blood, Heart, Respiration, and Renal Physiology.
