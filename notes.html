❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Are these BSc notes free?

Some notes are free to preview, and full semester-wise notes and PYQ sets unlock after signing in. Pricing, where applicable, is kept student-friendly.

Which DU syllabus are these notes based on?

Notes are aligned with the current Delhi University FYUP/NEP syllabus for each subject, and are updated as the syllabus changes each academic year.

Are the notes handwritten or typed?

Most notes are handwritten by the student contributor who prepared them, then scanned into clean, readable PDFs.

Do you cover previous year question papers?

Yes — Zoology and Microbiology both include DU previous year question papers alongside the semester notes. Botany's PYQ set is being prepared.

Can I contribute notes for my subject?

Yes — SayHeyShubh is built by a growing team of DU student contributors. Visit the Community page to find out how to join.
