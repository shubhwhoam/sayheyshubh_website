From Handwritten Notes to a DU Movement

SayHeyShubh wasn't engineered in a corporate boardroom. It was born in the classrooms of Deen Dayal Upadhyaya College. What started as one student sharing their handwritten Zoology notes has evolved into a centralized, peer-reviewed academic ecosystem serving thousands of Delhi University undergraduates.

The Scattered Syllabus Problem

With the implementation of the FYUP/NEP framework, DU students faced a massive challenge: textbooks were outdated, syllabus guidelines were confusing, and reliable, high-yield previous year questions (PYQs) were scattered across dozens of unverified internet portals. Freshers were spending more time searching for resources than actually studying.

The Centralized Solution

We built the bridge. SayHeyShubh consolidates verified, semester-wise handwritten notes, exact practical file methodologies, and solved PYQ databases into one sleek, highly accessible platform. We merged deep academic rigor with modern digital accessibility to ensure no DU science student ever has to study in the dark again.

Our Core Philosophy

Accessibility First

Education shouldn't be hidden behind massive paywalls or clunky websites. We prioritize delivering clean, easily downloadable, and beautifully formatted resources straight to your mobile screen.

Absolute Authenticity

No AI-generated fluff. No outsourced articles. Every single note on this platform is handwritten and verified by a Delhi University student who has successfully conquered the exact same syllabus.

Radical Empathy

College is stressful enough. We don't just provide academic notes; we foster an environment that openly discusses mental health, exam anxiety, and the real struggles of hostel life.
