About Me

Hi! I'm Shubham Kumar,

A BSc Zoology (Hons.) student at Deen Dayal Upadhyaya College, University of Delhi with a deep curiosity about science, and the intricate workings of the natural world. My academic journey is driven by a passion for understanding living organisms from microscopic life forms to complex ecosystems, and how they relate to human behavior, psychology, and creativity.

I am also a self-driven digital marketer and storyteller, dedicated to making complex scientific concepts accessible and engaging for my peers. This mission led me to create SayHeyShubh, a dedicated website, and a companion YouTube channel, Shubhiphilia. Both platforms serve as a hub for high-quality study materials and notes specifically tailored for BSc Zoology students. Building these platforms from the ground up has allowed me to merge my love for zoology with practical skills in web development, search engine optimization (SEO), and digital content creation. I’m actively learning digital marketing to expand my skill set and understand how impactful content, storytelling, and strategy can come together to drive awareness and change.

Open to opportunities that blend scientific exploration, digital marketing, strategic communication, and creative problem-solving.

It all started on YouTube — I began posting handwritten Zoology notes and study tips on my channel, Shubhiphilia, for fellow DU students. That grew into SayHeyShubh, and now into a small team of student contributors covering Zoology, Botany, and Microbiology.

💡 A Few Things About Me:

  • Create YouTube content around student life, study material, emotional support, and study motivation
  • Skilled in video editing (CapCut, Premiere Pro) & design (Canva, Photoshop, Adobe Express)
  • Completed a certificate in Emotional Intelligence (Grade: Outstanding)
  • Interested in Neuropsychology, mental health, and digital storytelling
  • Combine strategic SEO and content creation with hands-on web development skills to build and grow an online presence.

🎯 My Mission

To tell stories that make people feel seen, heard, and inspired. Whether it’s through a short video, a caption, or a conversation. I believe in creating content that connects deeply and honestly.
