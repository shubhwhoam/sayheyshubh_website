📋 DU Semester 1 Syllabus Overview

Official DU Syllabus

The first semester of BSc Zoology (Hons.) introduces students to the foundations of animal life. The core subjects (DSCs) include:

🦠 (DSC-1) Non Chordata: Protists to Pseudocoelomates. Covers introduction to Non-chordates, Protista, Porifera, Cnidaria, Ctenophora, Platyhelminthes and Nemathelminthes.
🔬 (DSC-2) Biology of Cell: Deep dive into Plasma membranes, Endomembrane System, Cytoskeleton, Nucleus, Cell Division, and Cell Signaling.
🌍 (DSC-3) Concepts of Ecology: Autecology, Synecology, Populations, Species Interactions, Communities, Ecosystems, and Applied Ecology.
