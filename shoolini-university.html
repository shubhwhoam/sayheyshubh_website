Research Briefing

Shoolini University: MSc Data Science

Compiled July 2026 · Sources: Official university website, Shiksha, CollegeDunia, Careers360, CollegeDekho, and news reports. Figures marked "official" come directly from the university; others are third-party estimates.

💰 1. Fees & Financials (AY 2026–27)

On-campus MSc Data Science & Analytics tuition rates:

Fee Structure HP / J&K Domicile Non-HP Domicile
Per Semester ₹66,750 ₹72,500
2-Year Total (Approx.) ~₹2.67 lakh ~₹2.90 lakh

Note: Tuition rises ~5% per year. A one-time ₹10,000 refundable academic security deposit also applies.

Hostel Fees (Per Semester):

  • Triple occupancy: ₹68,000 – ₹78,000
  • Double occupancy: ₹78,000 – ₹93,000
  • Full range: ₹38,000 – ₹1,02,000
Realistic 2-Year All-In Estimate:
Tuition + mid-tier hostel + deposit for a non-HP student comes to roughly ₹4.5–5.5 lakh total for the full MSc, before personal expenses and travel.

📍 2. Location & Campus Life

The campus is situated in the Kasauli Hills, Solan district, Himachal Pradesh (~1,500m elevation), surrounded by pine and deodar forests.

It sits about 11–12 km from Solan town, making it fairly isolated and not within walking distance of a city center.

The Trade-off: You get clean air, quiet, and an excellent environment for focus, but limited off-campus nightlife. Most social life is confined to the campus, which houses 9 cafeterias, a gym, a sports complex, a 24x7 library, and a cineplex.

📈 3. Placements Reality Check

⚖️ 4. Reviews Overview

Pros

  • Scenic, peaceful campus with strong lab infrastructure (104+ labs).
  • Approachable, industry-expert faculty with PhDs.
  • Strong scholarship program (up to 25–100% merit waivers).
  • Genuine international tie-ups (e.g., University of Melbourne).
  • Research-driven "One Student One Patent" initiative.

Cons

  • Placement quality is uneven; tech-adjacent courses fare better than others.
  • Hostel food is repeatedly described as merely "bearable".
  • Genuinely remote from any city, impacting weekend social expectations.
  • MSc Data Science lacks a long, independent placement track record.

⚠️ 5. Important Context: Recent News (April 2026)

1. Placement Dispute Investigation
MBA graduate Nitin Chauhan died by suicide on March 29, 2026, after allegedly facing a massive cut in a promised placement package without support from the placement cell . This triggered significant student protests. The university subsequently removed a senior official from her role and formed an internal inquiry panel.

2. Campus Altercation
A physical fight among female students went viral around the same period, prompting a disciplinary committee probe.

While isolated incidents happen at large institutions, the placement-related tragedy highlights the need to ask admissions directly about how placement offers are verified and what safeguards exist for students.

📋 6. Due Diligence Checklist

  • Curriculum: Verify it includes Python, SQL, Statistics, ML, Deep Learning, NLP, Big Data, and a capstone project.
  • Faculty: Review faculty profiles on LinkedIn/Google Scholar.
  • Internships: Ask about the percentage of paid vs. unpaid internships and partner companies.
  • Alumni: Speak to at least three current students or alumni.
  • Hidden Costs: Confirm exam fees, deposits, laptop requirements, and transport charges.

Final Verdict

Shoolini University offers a strong campus environment and research focus, but the biggest unknown is programme-specific placement performance. Admission is a reasonable choice only after verifying recent MSc Data Science placement statistics, curriculum relevance, and internship support directly with current students and the administration.
