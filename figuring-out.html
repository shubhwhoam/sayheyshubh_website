I don't have to have it all figured out 🍂

Written by Shubham Kumar • August 20, 2025
Well... I don't have to have it all figured out. I don't have to worry about what my career will carry, who, when, or if I'll get married. I don't have to feel like I'm running out of time, Because real genuine life exists between the lines, And I haven't even lived before. This is the first time. That's why I'm so twisted, cuz everything I feel like I should've done by now I've listed. But what's that even based off, other versions of myself that never existed? Don't even get me started on comparing yourself to others. Cuz everything online Is designed To make you feel behind. Everyone has setbacks, problems, and pain, Even if you swap with them you'll feel the same. So what's my purpose? Why I'm here? I don't know. But I know we're not here to just try to figure out why we're here. And I don't see much purpose in just trying to figure out what's my purpose. You just gotta do.. go out and do things. So... I'll say it again in case you doubt, You don't have to have it all figured out.
