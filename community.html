More Than Just Making PDFs

Wondering what it's actually like to be part of the SayHeyShubh team? We keep it flexible, highly impactful, and student-first.

Zero Corporate Pressure

You are a full-time student first. We totally get the pressure of assignment deadlines, society fests, and internals. You contribute strictly at your own pace—no rigid office hours.

Real Impact, Real Fast

When you finish a module, we hit publish. It is deeply rewarding to log in and instantly watch your notes helping hundreds of juniors prep for their exams overnight.

A Supportive Inner Circle

We do more than just upload notes. We share premium resources, review each other's work, and occasionally vent about DU's chaotic administration together in our group chat.
