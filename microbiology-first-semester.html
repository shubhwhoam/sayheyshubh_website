📋 BSc Microbiology (Hons.) Semester 1 Syllabus Overview

The first semester introduces the foundations of microbiology and biochemistry. The core papers are:

🔬 (MICROB-DSC101) Introduction to Microbial World: History and scope of microbiology, and an overview of the microbial world.
🦠 (MICROB-DSC102) Basic Bacteriology: Bacterial structure, growth, and fundamental bacteriology concepts.
🧬 (MICROB-DSC103) Principles of Biochemistry-I: Foundational biochemistry concepts for microbiology students.
