Frequently Asked Questions

What is SayHeyShubh?

SayHeyShubh is a completely student-driven peer-to-peer education repository designed explicitly for Delhi University students to gain premium access to undergrad science notes and community systems.

Is SayHeyShubh officially affiliated with Delhi University?

No. SayHeyShubh is a completely independent student project created by Shubham Kumar. It holds no official administrative affiliations with the university administration.
