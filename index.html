❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What is SayHeyShubh?

SayHeyShubh is a student-driven learning community for Delhi University students — starting with BSc Zoology and growing to cover Botany and Microbiology, built by DU students for DU students.

Is SayHeyShubh officially affiliated with Delhi University?

No — SayHeyShubh is an independent, student-run platform. It's not officially affiliated with Delhi University or any specific college, but it's built by and for DU students.

Which subjects are currently covered?

Zoology and Microbiology are fully live with semester-wise notes and previous year question papers. Botany is being prepared by a student contributor and will go live subject-by-subject.

How can I become a contributor?

If you're a DU student who takes great notes and wants to help other students, visit the Community page to find out how to join.
